Do not forget sound

Mon, Oct 13, 2014

If you are creating a game without an audio budget, it does not mean it won’t have sound. A game without sound does not make for a great mood.

Even if sound effects aren’t your forte the what you can do is voice them yourself (which can be quite funny), look on opengameart or generate sounds using tools like bfxr.
